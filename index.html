About Us


We specialize in baking a traditional New York style pizza.
with a thin (but not a cracker) crust
a slice of our pizza is meant to be folded in half lengthwise and eaten New York style. Add our slightly sweet and spicy pizza sauce,
our own spacial blend of top quality Mozarella and Provolone Cheese,
and your choice of fresh toppings,
you have a combination that will have you falling in love with pizza all over again.

We're different. We like to think its in a "good way".

STARTERS

Garlic Knots

15 mini rolls baked to perfection, brushed with a side of our marinera sauce. So good we should have named the whole place after them.

Primo Garlic Cheese Bread

1/2 loaf of our home made focaccia bread, buttered and covered in cheese, lightly baked.

Side Salad

Classic house salad with freshly torn romaine lettuce, slightly topped with pepperoni, black olives, roma tomatoes, croutons, and cheese. Served with a dressing of your choice.

Greek Salad

Share a starter or meal for one. Savory salad combo featuring pepperoni, green and black olives, roma tomatoes, croutons, pepperonchinis, and topped with fresh feta cheese.

PIZZA / ENTREES

Super Combo

Our most popular Specialty pizza. A variation of the works, generously topped with pepperonis, italian sausage, green pepper, onion, and black olives.

Hawaiian BBQ

Sweet baby rays goes to the big apple; we've got the bbq sauce, ham, pineapples, and chopped chicken breast. Coming together all for your mouth pleasure.

Deep Dish

9 inch traditional Chicago style deep dish with extra sauce, your choice of upto five pizza toppings, extra cheese, and slowly baked to perfection.

Calzone

Four - cheeses mixed together, with your choice of up-to 5 toppings. Stuffed into a half moon (shaped) pocket of our freshly made pizza dough.

Baked Ziti

Penna noodles mixed together with a four cheese blend. In marinera topped with your choice of: meatballs, italian sausage, or chicken breast. Heaped with extra cheese and baked to a golden brown.

DESSERTS

Canolli

Sweetened ricotta and mascarpone cheeses, mixed with mini choclate chips, all stuffed into a crispy pastry shell.

Dessert Breadsticks

A less twisted version of our dessert knots!

Dessert Knots

15 Freshly baked mini rolls sprinkled with a delicious cinnamon and powder sugar blend. Served with our own sweetened cheese cake batter.

Cheese Cake

A delicious slice of our new york style cheese cake.
